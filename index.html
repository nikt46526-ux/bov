How Kids Learn with Creative Coloring

1. Pick a Picture

Your child can choose from a gallery of drawing templates. Starting with a pre-drawn image helps them understand shapes and composition as they prepare to add color.

2. Choose Your Colors

Next, they select colors from the palette. This step teaches them about color names and encourages them to think about which colors will look best together in their drawing.

3. Create a Masterpiece

As they fill in the drawing, children experiment with color combinations and develop their artistic style. This process builds confidence and improves their creative decision-making skills.

4. Save and Share

Once their drawing is complete, you can save it to your device. This allows you to track their progress and share their achievements, reinforcing their new skills.
